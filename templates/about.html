About Deep Doc Ai

Welcome to Deep Doc Ai, your personal AI-powered assistant for reading, analyzing, and chatting with your PDF documents.

Our Mission

We built Deep Doc Ai to solve a simple problem: too much reading, not enough time. Whether you're a student dealing with massive textbooks, a researcher combing through dense papers, or a professional reviewing lengthy contracts, our tool uses state-of-the-art AI to help you find the information you need, instantly.

How It Works

When you upload a PDF, we securely extract its text, break it down into manageable chunks, and generate deep semantic representations (embeddings). Then, when you ask a question, our Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) engine scans your document to find the most relevant sections and uses an advanced Large Language Model to formulate a precise, contextual answer based only on your file.
